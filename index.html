The Akan name generator!

Hello! This web application takes your birthday details and calculates the day of the week you were born. It then outputs your Akan Name depending on your gender.

This is a Ghanian culture where children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.

Want to know your Akan Name?

Fill in your birthday and gender then click Generate Akan Name.
